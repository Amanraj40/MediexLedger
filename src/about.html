About MediexLedger

Mediexledger is a web platform dedicated to storing information about medicines used in our hospital. We provide a comprehensive database of medicines, including details such as who is manufacturer,retailer,seller,customer of that Medicine and more.

Our platform also offers a tracking system that allows customers to check whether the medicine is real or duplicate vis qr code on the packet. This helps ensure that essential medicines are always available when needed.

Moreover, each medicine in our database is associated with a unique QR code, which can be scanned to verify its identity and authenticity. This feature enhances safety and security in medication management.

This also helps in reducing counterfitting of medicines
